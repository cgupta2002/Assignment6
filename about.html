My Story

Born and raised in suburban Illinois, I was always a nerdy and passionate child. I built my first PC at 15 and I've been coding since I can remember. I started with Scratch and have grown to learn many different languages and use cases throughout the years. I'm a diehard White Sox fan, no matter how difficult things may be, and I frequently go to games when I can. I also enjoy playing Tennis and Pickleball with friends during my free time. I'm also a massive gamer, though the catalog has been less diverse recently due to less free time.



Academic Career


My undergraduate degrees were in Information Systems and Business Analytics from the Kelley School of Business, alongside a Certificate in Informatics from the Luddy School of Informatics, Engineering and Computing.
